Public High School Data Set

State Asssements Data (EDFACTS)

Contains district-level achievement results for state assessments in mathematics and reading or language arts, by grade. It includes the number of students who completed each assessment for whom a proficiency level was assigned and the proficiency share. The proficiency share is reported as a range, unless there are more than 300 students in the subgroup, with the magnitude of the range decreasing as the number of students reported increases. States can change their statewide assessments, academic standards, or thresholds for proficiency levels, leading to changes in the proficiency share from year to year.
